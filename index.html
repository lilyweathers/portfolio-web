About me

I’m a Web Developer and Tech Administrator with practical experience in building modern, responsive applications using JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and popular frontend frameworks. Alongside this, I bring a solid foundation in Java and Spring for backend development, giving me the ability to contribute across the full stack. During my professional work and internships, I’ve designed and developed responsive websites, improved SEO performance, enhanced user experience, and collaborated with cross-functional teams to deliver reliable, commercial-grade web solutions. By working closely with designers, backend developers, and stakeholders, I’ve gained a strong understanding of how to translate business needs into effective digital products. My background extends beyond pure development. With experience in administration, media production, and customer-facing roles, I bring creativity, organizational skills, and clear communication into every project. This diverse perspective allows me to approach technical challenges with both analytical and user-centric thinking. I’m passionate about building scalable, user-friendly applications and thrive in collaborative environments where knowledge-sharing and problem-solving are at the forefront. Constantly learning and adapting, I’m eager to take on complex challenges that push my skills further while delivering meaningful solutions.
